About AISU

Where authentic flavors meet Swiss hospitality

Our Story

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Switzerland, AISU was born from a passion for bringing authentic Italian cuisine to Swiss tables. Our name "AISU" reflects our commitment to excellence - combining the warmth of Italian hospitality with Swiss precision and quality.

What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved restaurant, but we've never forgotten our roots. Every dish is prepared with the same love and attention to detail that our founders brought to their first meal.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional dining experiences by combining traditional Italian recipes with the finest Swiss ingredients. We believe that great food brings people together, and every meal should be a celebration of flavor, quality, and community.

Our Values

  • Quality: We source only the finest ingredients, supporting local Swiss farmers and authentic Italian suppliers.
  • Tradition: Our recipes honor time-tested Italian cooking methods passed down through generations.
  • Innovation: While respecting tradition, we're not afraid to add our own creative touch to classic dishes.
  • Community: We're proud to be part of the Swiss community and give back whenever possible.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At AISU, we believe in responsible dining. We work closely with local Swiss farmers, minimize food waste through careful planning, and use eco-friendly packaging for our delivery services. Our commitment to the environment is as strong as our commitment to great food.
